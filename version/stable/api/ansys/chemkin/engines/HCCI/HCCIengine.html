HCCIengine.set_energy_equation_switch_ON_CA(switchCA: float)#

Set the crank angle at which the energy equation will be turn ON for the rest of the simulation. Before this switch crank angle the given temperature profile(s) or value(s) is used in the multi-zone HCCI simulation.

Parameters#

switchCA: double

energy equation activation crank angle [degree]
